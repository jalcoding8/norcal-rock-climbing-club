If you're just starting, wear comfortable clothes you can move in easily.
You will need climbing shoes, which can be rented at any climbing gym.
You can also rent a harness for top rope climbing.
For outdoor climbing you'll need a climbing helmet. Other members will bring the "crash" pads.

If you want to top rope or lead climb with our group you must be certified, accomplished at a climbing gym.
For this you'll need your own harness.
